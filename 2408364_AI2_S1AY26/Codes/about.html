About Rugs Unlimited

Rugs Unlimited is an online store that helps you find the perfect rug for every room. We offer a handpicked selection of area rugs, runners, and accent mats in many styles, from modern minimal to classic hand-knotted designs. Our rugs come in various sizes to fit living rooms, bedrooms, hallways, and entryways. We focus on quality and clear sizing guidance. Our excellent customer service includes easy returns and reliable shipping, so you can shop with confidence.
